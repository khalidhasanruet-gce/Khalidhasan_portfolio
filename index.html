Selected Research Projects

A focused selection of research work on advanced ceramics, functional oxides, and thin-film materials.

Multiferroic Ceramics

Bismuth Ferrite (BiFeO₃) Ceramics

Investigated the synthesis and characterization of BiFeO₃ ceramics using the solid-state route, with emphasis on phase formation, microstructure evolution, and electrical/dielectric performance in multifunctional oxide systems.

Thin Films

Zinc Oxide (ZnO) Thin Films

Worked on fabrication and characterization of ZnO thin films for optoelectronic applications, focusing on structural quality, optical transparency, and surface morphology.

Optical transparency Crystallinity Band behavior Surface uniformity

Studied the dielectric and microstructural behavior of CCTO ceramics with emphasis on grain boundary effects, electrical response, and giant dielectric properties.
