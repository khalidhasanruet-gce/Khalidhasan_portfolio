About

Research profile shaped by structure, processing, and performance

My academic and industrial work centers on understanding how composition, processing, and microstructure influence the behavior of advanced ceramic and oxide materials. I aim to develop materials systems that are both scientifically meaningful and application-oriented.

Research Identity

A materials science researcher focused on ceramics, thin films, functional oxides, and characterization-driven analysis.

Current Work

In industry, I contribute to process optimization, defect reduction, material efficiency, and quality improvement in ceramic production.

Long-Term Direction

My long-term goal is doctoral research in advanced materials, energy materials, nanotechnology, and ceramic additive manufacturing.
